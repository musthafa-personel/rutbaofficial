RUTBA

The Reviving Union for Transcendent and Benevolent Activities of Fathima Zahra Islamic Women's College under the Gazva Union. We are a vibrant community of students dedicated to personal growth, academic excellence, and service to society.

Founded with the vision of empowering young women to become leaders of tomorrow, RUTBA serves as a platform for students to develop their talents, expand their horizons, and make meaningful contributions to the world around them.

Our union embraces the values of compassion, knowledge, integrity, and service, guiding our members to excel not only in academic pursuits but also in character development and community engagement.

Our Mission

🌱

Personal Growth

Fostering intellectual, spiritual, and personal development of our members through educational programs and character-building activities.

🤝

Community Service

Organizing initiatives that address social issues and contribute positively to the welfare of our community and society at large.

🎯

Leadership

Developing leadership skills and qualities that enable our members to become agents of positive change in their communities.

Our Activities

RUTBA organizes a diverse range of activities throughout the academic year, providing opportunities for learning, service, and fellowship.

Educational Workshops

Interactive sessions on academic subjects, career guidance, and life skills to enhance personal and professional development.

Community Outreach

Initiatives focused on serving the underprivileged, environmental conservation, and addressing social issues.

Cultural Programs

Events celebrating diverse cultural heritages, fostering appreciation for art, literature, and traditions.

Leadership Training

Programs designed to nurture leadership qualities, communication skills, and teamwork among members.

Spiritual Development

Sessions focused on spiritual growth, ethics, and values that guide personal conduct and decision-making.

Health & Wellness

Activities promoting physical health, mental wellbeing, and healthy lifestyle choices.
